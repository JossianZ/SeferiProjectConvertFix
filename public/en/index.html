Core Features

Ensure Full Compliance

Document processing is typically cumbersome, but Seferi's artificial intelligence ensures that all your shipments comply fully with both local and international regulations. This reduces the risk of penalties, shipment delays, and other compliance-related issues.

Increase Transparency and Control

With Seferi's real-time shipment tracking, you will always have complete visibility over your logistics operations. This not only helps you monitor progress and intervene in potential delays but also enhances customer satisfaction by providing accurate delivery updates.

Real-Time Monitoring and Learning

Seferi allows you to track your shipments in real-time while continuously improving logistics processes based on past data and current conditions. With detailed and regular reporting, it becomes easier for you to make informed and proactive decisions in operational management.

Optimize Your Logistics Operations

Seferi automates and optimizes the most complex parts of your logistics operations, from route planning to document management. Thanks to real-time adjustments and smart resource allocations, Seferi helps you reduce costs, prevent delays, and deliver shipments faster and more efficiently.

Eliminate Human Errors

Manual logistics processes are prone to errors, and these errors can lead to delays, incorrect deliveries, and unnecessary costs. Seferi's AI-powered automation minimizes human errors by ensuring that every route, vehicle assignment, and document processing is handled with precision.

Increase Fleet Productivity

Seferi's smart vehicle assignments allow you to utilize your fleet's full potential, matching shipments with the most suitable vehicles based on capacity, load type, and distance. This means fewer empty vehicles, better resource utilization, and a more efficient logistics operation.

How Seferi Works: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Log in to your Account

Access the Seferi control panel by logging in with your credentials. Field users can log in through the mobile application, customers can enter the customer portal, and operational managers can access the main control panel from the Seferi desktop application.

Contact us to open an account

Step 2: Set the Basic Information

Go to the Admin Panel section in the control panel to enter your routine tasks, vehicles, and other information that will help Seferi customize your experience. These adjustments allow Seferi to optimize your shipments accurately.

Contact us to open an account

Step 3: Place Your First Order

To create your first order, go to the New Order section. Enter the shipping information, cargo specifications, and other relevant details. Then click the Confirm button to save the order. With the customer portal, your customers can easily place orders in your system.

Contact us to open an account

Step 4: Review and Plan Your Order

Your order will appear on the Unscheduled tab after it has been confirmed. When you click on the order, Seferi AI will automatically assign the best route and vehicle based on the order details. Save the scheduled order and continue.

Contact us to open an account

Step 5: Real-Time Shipment Tracking and Management

Seferi allows you to monitor every stage of the shipment process from dispatch to delivery. You can view active shipments using the Vehicle Tracking map and track progress at every stage.

Contact us to open an account
